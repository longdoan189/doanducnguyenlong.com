Working experience

From July 2023 to September 2024 I was a Technical Engineer and later Technical Advisor at iTechwx. My role was working with customers to resolve complex technical customer issues on the Microsoft Azure Platform as well as Windows Server that related to authentication process (in simplier words: IT mates have problem with their user's login and I help them). This role is interesting and allow me to understand application of cybersecurity into industrial products as well as understand the IT industry in general from customer.

From June 2021 to August 2021 (during my bachelor's summer vacation), I had an internship at CoderSchool to develop an automated grading system for machine learning courses organised by the institution to help students' assessment easier. Previously, lecturers have to grade student's homework by hand for every student. With this autograder, this can be done en masse, allowing the class to scale up.

In May 2021, I was a lecturer at Code in Place. This online course was offered by Stanford University during the COVID-19. As a volunteer section leader, I prepared and taught a weekly discussion section of 8-10 students to supplement professors' lectures.

From October 2020 to April 2021, I was a math mentor for Fulbright University Vietnam. My role was to help student (both with or without math background) to understand the concepts and be able to apply it for their study.
